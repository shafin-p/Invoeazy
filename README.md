## 📱 Download the App

1. Go to the **[Actions tab](https://github.com/shafin-p/Invoeazy/actions)** above
2. Click the latest ✅ green build called **"Build Android APK"**
3. Scroll down to **"Artifacts"** and click **`Invoeazy-App-APK`**
4. Extract the downloaded `.zip` file to get your `.apk`
5. Transfer it to your Android phone and install it

> On your phone: **Settings → Security → Allow from Unknown Sources** before installing.

## ✨ What You Can Do With Invoeazy

### 🏪 Set Up Your Shop
- Register with your shop name, type, and details
- Choose from shop types: Kirana, Medical, Restaurant, Hardware, and more
- Get a pre-loaded product catalog based on your shop type

### 🛒 Manage Inventory
- Add, edit, and delete products easily
- Set prices and units (kg, litre, strips, dozens, pieces, etc.)
- Quick access to your full product list during billing

### 🧾 Create Bills in Seconds
- Search and add products to cart instantly
- Enter quantity in any unit — price is calculated automatically
  - Example: Product price is ₹100/kg → customer buys 250 grams → total = ₹25 ✅
- Add a product on-the-fly even if it's not in your list
- Customer name and phone number (optional)

### 📲 Share QR Digital Bills
- Every bill generates a unique **QR code**
- Customer scans it with their phone camera — no app needed
- They can view and save a clean, professional digital receipt
- Works completely offline on your device

### 📖 Khata (Credit) Book
- Mark any bill as **Khata** (credit/udhaar)
- Balance is tracked automatically per customer
- View how much each customer owes you

### 📊 Revenue at a Glance (Home Screen)
- See **Today's Bills**, **Today's Revenue**, and **Monthly Revenue** at a glance
- Displayed right on the home screen every time you open the app

### 🎨 Customize Your Bill Design
- Change the bill color, font style, and layout
- Upload your shop logo to appear on every receipt
- Choose between Modern Clean or Classic Thermal receipt styles

## 🆓 Free & Works Offline

- **No internet needed** for billing — all data is stored on your phone
- **No monthly fees** — completely free to use
- Works on **any Android phone**
- Bills and Khata records are **saved permanently** on your device
